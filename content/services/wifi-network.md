## Common Problems I Fix

### "My WiFi is slow"

Nine times out of ten, it's one of these:

- **Router placement** — it's in a closet, behind the TV, or in the worst possible corner of the house
- **Channel congestion** — your neighbors' routers are all fighting for the same frequency
- **Old hardware** — that router from 2016 can't keep up with 2026 devices
- **ISP issues** — sometimes it really is their fault, and I can help you prove it
- **Too many devices** — smart TVs, phones, tablets, cameras, thermostats... they add up

### "The WiFi doesn't reach my [room]"

Dead zones happen. Solutions include:

- **Optimal router repositioning** — sometimes just moving it 10 feet fixes everything
- **Mesh WiFi systems** — multiple access points that work as one seamless network
- **WiFi extenders** — almost always make things worse and should be avoided at all costs

### "My network is a mess"

Rogue gateways, double-NAT, unknown devices, printers that work sometimes — I'll untangle it and set up something clean.

## What You Should Know

- **WiFi 6/6E** is worth upgrading to if your router is more than 3-4 years old
- **Mesh > Extenders** in almost every scenario (extenders halve your bandwidth)
- **Your ISP's router** is usually garbage. Buying your own modem + router saves money long-term and gives you better performance
- **Ethernet is still king** — for gaming, streaming 4K, or working from home, nothing beats a cable
