## How It Works

1. **You reach out** — call, email, or use the contact form
2. **I send you a link** — click it in any browser, no software to install
3. **I connect to your screen** — you can see everything I'm doing
4. **We fix it together** — I diagnose, explain, and resolve

The whole process usually takes 15–45 minutes. You don't need to drop your computer off anywhere or wait for someone to drive to your house.

## What I Can Fix Remotely

- **Slow computer** — identify what's eating your resources and clean it up
- **Virus/malware identification** — find out once and for all if you can ignore that warning or if it's something to worry about
- **Software issues** — crashes, error messages, programs that won't install or update
- **Email problems** — setup, recovery, sync issues across devices
- **Printer headaches** — drivers, connectivity, "why won't it just print"
- **General "something's not right"** — you don't need to know the technical term, just describe what's happening

## What I Can't Fix Remotely

Some things need hands-on. OS reinstalls, networking issues, malware removal, etc. For anything like that, we'll schedule an on-site visit or you can bring it to me.

## What It Costs

**$60/hour**, billed in 15-minute increments. Most remote sessions land between $30–$60.

No diagnostic fee. No minimum charge beyond the first 15 minutes. If I connect and realize it's a 2-minute fix, I'll tell you — and charge accordingly.

## Why Remote First?

Through the wonder of technology I can assist in a matter of milliseconds. If I can't fix, I can diagnose. We both save time and gas, everyone wins.
