## Upgrades & Refurbs

Sometimes you don't need a new computer. You need the right part in the old one.

- **RAM upgrades** — the single biggest bang for your buck on older machines
- **SSD swaps** — turning a 5-minute boot into a 15-second boot
- **GPU upgrades** — for when your kid discovers PC gaming
- **Thermal paste & cooling** — if your laptop sounds like a jet engine
- **Full refurb** — clean install, new thermal paste, clean the fans, max the RAM, SSD swap. Like new for a fraction of the price.
