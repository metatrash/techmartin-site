## What This Covers

### System Backups & Data Protection

Your photos, documents, tax records, that one folder you haven't organized since 2017 — all of it matters. I'll set up a backup system that actually works and runs without you thinking about it.

- **Local backups** — external drives, NAS setups
- **Cloud backups** — choosing the right service and configuring it properly
- **Backup verification** — making sure your backups are actually recoverable (most people never check)
- **Data migration** — old computer to new computer, keeping everything intact

### OS Upgrades & System Migration

Still on Windows 10? Need to move from an old PC to a new one? Upgrade while making sure your software, files, and settings come with you.

- **Windows** — clean installs or in-place, depending on what makes sense
- **Linux** — if you're curious, I can help you try it safely.

### Security Audits (Home & Small Business)

You don't need a Fortune 500 security budget to protect yourself. Most breaches happen because of simple stuff:

- **Password audit** — are you reusing passwords? Using "password123"? Let's fix that.
- **Network security** — is your WiFi open? Is your router running default firmware from 2019?
- **Device hardening** — making sure your machines aren't broadcasting to the world
- **Phishing awareness** — I'll show you what real scam emails look like so you stop falling for them
- **Smart home review** — that cheap camera from Amazon might be phoning home to somewhere you don't want
